Understanding the Auto Sweep Facility

The auto sweep facility, also known as the Multi Option Deposit Scheme (MODS), is a unique feature provided by SBI. It allows you to maintain a high balance in your savings account while automatically transferring excess funds into fixed deposits, which earn higher interest rates. This way, you can maximize your returns without compromising on liquidity.

Step-by-Step Guide to Activate Auto Sweep Facility for SBI Account

Step 1: Carefully Read the Terms and Conditions

Before activating the auto sweep facility, it is essential to familiarize yourself with the terms and conditions associated with this feature. Carefully read through the terms and conditions provided by SBI to ensure you understand the implications and benefits of using the auto sweep facility.

Step 2: Log in to SBI Online Banking

To activate the auto sweep facility, you need to access your SBI account through online banking. Visit the SBI official website and enter your login credentials to log in to your account securely.

Step 3: Deposit & Investment

Once you have logged in, navigate to the “Deposit & Investment” section. This section typically contains Deposit & Investment services related to your SBI account, including various account modification options.

Step 4: Locate and Select Auto Sweep Facility

Within the Deposite & Investment Section section, locate and select the option for the “Auto Sweep Facility” or “Multi Option Deposit Scheme.” This option may vary depending on the specific interface of SBI’s online banking platform.

Here you will see your account information like Name, Address, Branche address, etc Now you need to click on the checkbox located where the account number showing then click on Continue.

Step 4: Enter High-Security Password.

After Clicking on Continue You will get a High Security Password On your registered mobile number enter it and click on Conform button.

Step 5: Submit Auto Sweep Facility Details.

Now a New Window will open in front of Your Screen here you need to enter a few more details like Start date, sweep cycle, thousand amount, and resultant balance, and finally click on submit button your Sbi auto sweep facility will start in your account.

Step 6: Read the Terms and Conditions

Before activating the auto sweep facility, it is essential to familiarize yourself with the terms and conditions associated with this feature. Carefully read through the terms and conditions provided by SBI to ensure you understand the implications and benefits of using the auto sweep facility.

Step 7: Agree to the Terms and Conditions

Once you have thoroughly reviewed the terms and conditions, click on the “Agree” or “Accept” button to proceed with activating the auto sweep facility for your SBI account. By doing so, you confirm that you understand and agree to comply with the specified guidelines and rules.

Step 8: Set the Threshold Amount

Next, you will be prompted to set the threshold amount for the auto sweep facility. The threshold amount determines the limit above which excess funds from your savings account will be automatically transferred into fixed deposits. Choose an appropriate threshold amount that suits your financial goals and preferences.

Step 9: Confirm and Activate

After setting the threshold amount, review the details you have entered to ensure accuracy. Once you are satisfied with the information provided, click on the “Confirm” or “Activate” button to activate the auto sweep facility for your SBI account.

Benefits of Activating Auto Sweep Facility

By activating the auto sweep facility for your SBI account, you can enjoy numerous benefits, including:

  • Higher Interest Rates: The auto sweep facility enables you to earn higher interest rates on your excess funds by automatically transferring them into fixed deposits.
  • Liquidity and Convenience: You can maintain a high balance in your savings account while enjoying the convenience of automatic fund transfers, ensuring that your funds are readily accessible when needed.
  • Efficient Fund Management: The auto sweep facility optimizes your funds by distributing them between your savings account and fixed deposits, maximizing both liquidity and returns.
  • No Manual Intervention: Once you have set the threshold amount and activated the auto sweep facility, the process of transferring funds into fixed deposits is automated, eliminating the need for manual intervention.

FAQ(Frequently Asked Questions) about SBI Auto Sweep Facility?

Can I set my threshold amount for the Auto Sweep Facility?

Yes, as an account holder, you have the flexibility to set your threshold amount for the Auto Sweep Facility. Consider your financial goals and preferences while setting the threshold amount to ensure it aligns with your requirements.

Q2: Is there a minimum balance requirement for activating the Auto Sweep Facility?

A2: Yes, there is generally a minimum balance requirement to activate the Auto Sweep Facility. The specific minimum balance may vary based on the type of account and other factors. It is recommended to check with SBI or refer to the account terms and conditions for precise information regarding the minimum balance requirement.

Q3: Can I deactivate the Auto Sweep Facility if I change my mind?

A3: Yes, the Auto Sweep Facility can be deactivated upon your request. You can contact SBI customer service or visit your nearest branch to initiate the deactivation process.

Q4: Is the Auto Sweep Facility available for all types of SBI accounts?

A4: The availability of the Auto Sweep Facility may vary depending on the type of SBI account you hold. It is advisable to check with SBI or refer to the account terms and conditions to determine if the facility applies to your specific account.

Q5: Can I modify the threshold amount for the Auto Sweep Facility after activation?

A5: Yes, you can modify the threshold amount for the Auto Sweep Facility after activation. However, it is important to note that any changes to the threshold amount may have an impact on the fund transfer mechanism and the overall operation of the facility. It is recommended to review the terms and conditions and consult with SBI for guidance before making any modifications.
